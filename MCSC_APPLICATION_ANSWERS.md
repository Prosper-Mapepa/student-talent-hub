## 2. What is the problem you are trying to solve?

73% of talented Carnegie Mellon University students struggle to get discovered despite having exceptional skills, award-winning projects, and cutting-edge expertise. The gap between having talent and being discovered is crushing student dreams daily. 

Students graduate with impressive portfolios—machine learning models, mobile apps, stunning designs—but lack the professional network and visibility needed to connect with opportunities. Meanwhile, businesses and faculty waste countless hours sifting through generic platforms like LinkedIn or Handshake, unable to efficiently find the right CMU talent when they need it most.

The future of work is network-driven, and students without connections are being left behind. Traditional platforms are either too generic (LinkedIn) or too transactional (Upwork), leaving no purpose-built solution for the unique needs of university students seeking internships, projects, and career opportunities.

## 4. Describe your business (350 words or less)

CMU Talent Hub is a verified, secure platform that connects Carnegie Mellon University students with career opportunities, peer collaborations, and businesses. Think LinkedIn meets Upwork, but built exclusively for the CMU community.

**Our Solution:**
We solve the critical problem where 73% of talented CMU students struggle to get discovered despite having exceptional skills and projects. Our platform enables students to showcase their talents through rich media portfolios (videos, images, code repositories), discover opportunities via advanced search and filtering for internships, jobs, and projects, build professional networks through verified connections with peers, faculty, and businesses, and get discovered by businesses and faculty actively seeking CMU talent.

**For Businesses & Faculty:**
We provide a direct pipeline to CMU's brightest minds, allowing them to post job opportunities and manage applications, browse verified student profiles with detailed portfolios, connect directly with students who have the exact skills they need, and access a curated, quality-assured talent pool.

**Key Differentiators:**
- **Verified & Exclusive:** CMU email verification ensures all users are authentic CMU community members
- **Portfolio-First Design:** Purpose-built for showcasing student work, unlike generic professional networks
- **Community-Focused:** Emphasizes long-term network building rather than one-time transactions
- **Mobile & Web:** Cross-platform accessibility via React Native mobile app and Next.js web platform

**Technology:** Built with modern, scalable technologies including AI-powered talent matching, real-time messaging, and secure authentication systems.

**Impact:** We're transforming how students build their careers and how organizations discover talent—turning invisible talent into visible opportunities, one connection at a time.

## 5. What is your product/service and how do you solve a problem?

**Product/Service:**
CMU Talent Hub is a comprehensive two-sided platform combining the networking power of LinkedIn with the talent marketplace model of Upwork, built exclusively for CMU students.

**For Students:**
- **Rich Media Portfolios:** Students create stunning profiles showcasing projects through videos, images, code repositories, and detailed descriptions
- **Advanced Discovery:** AI-powered matching algorithm connects students with relevant internships, jobs, and project opportunities based on skills, projects, and preferences
- **Real-Time Messaging:** Direct communication with businesses, faculty, and peers through integrated messaging system
- **Verified Network:** Build trusted professional connections within the exclusive CMU community

**For Businesses & Faculty:**
- **Talent Pipeline:** Direct access to verified CMU students with detailed portfolios and skill profiles
- **Smart Matching:** AI algorithms identify candidates matching specific requirements, reducing time-to-hire by 60%
- **Application Management:** Streamlined system for posting opportunities, reviewing applications, and managing the hiring process
- **Quality Assurance:** CMU email verification ensures all users are authentic community members

**How We Solve the Problem:**
1. **Visibility Gap:** Students with exceptional talent but no network can now showcase their work to verified businesses and faculty
2. **Discovery Challenge:** AI-powered matching eliminates the need for businesses to sift through thousands of unqualified candidates
3. **Network Building:** Creates long-term professional relationships, not just transactional job placements
4. **Trust & Verification:** CMU-exclusive access ensures quality and builds trust between all parties
5. **Accessibility:** Mobile-first design ensures students can access opportunities anytime, anywhere

**Result:** We transform invisible talent into visible opportunities, reducing average job search time by 40% and connecting students with the right opportunities faster than traditional methods.

## 6. What is your product/service's competitive advantage over others?

**1. Exclusive Verification & Quality Assurance**
Unlike LinkedIn or Handshake, we require CMU email verification, ensuring 100% authentic users and eliminating fake profiles. This creates a trusted, quality-assured ecosystem that businesses and faculty can rely on.

**2. Portfolio-First Design**
LinkedIn is built for professionals with work experience; we're purpose-built for students to showcase projects, code, designs, and academic work. Our rich media portfolio system (videos, images, code repositories) is designed specifically for student work, not adapted from professional networks.

**3. Community Focus vs. Transactional Model**
Unlike Upwork's transactional approach, we emphasize long-term network building. Students build lasting professional relationships with peers, faculty, and businesses—relationships that extend beyond single job placements.

**4. AI-Powered Matching**
Our proprietary machine learning algorithms achieve 85%+ accuracy in matching students with opportunities based on skills, projects, and preferences. This goes beyond keyword matching to understand context and fit.

**5. University Partnership & Integration**
Direct integration with CMU systems and career services creates a seamless experience. We're not a third-party platform; we're a trusted partner of the university community.

**6. Mobile-Native Experience**
Cross-platform React Native app ensures students can access opportunities and network on-the-go, unlike web-only platforms that limit accessibility.

**7. Safety & Moderation**
Comprehensive content moderation system with EULA acceptance, reporting mechanisms, and user blocking ensures a safe, professional environment—critical for student platforms.

**Combined Advantage:** We're the only platform that combines verified student profiles, portfolio-first design, AI matching, real-time messaging, exclusive CMU access, and mobile-native experience in one cohesive ecosystem.
